ABOUT THE GAME

  • LIG-4 is an exciting challenge and strategy game.
  • It's easy to learn and fun to play.
  • Its rules are simple: each player tries to place four of his stones in a row inside the board, either horizontally, vertically or diagonally, blocking his opponent so that he cannot do the same.
  • It's a fast and super fun throwing game.!
  • The objective is to line up 4 tiles of your color. It can be horizontally, vertically or diagonally. - Were you the first to line up 4 chips??? Congratulations! You won!
